9. Job Hunting and Interview Techniques

To land a job in front-end development, you need to:

Building Your Portfolio

  • Showcase your projects on GitHub.
  • Create a personal portfolio website using the technologies you've learned.

Job Hunting Strategies

  • Networking: Attend meetups, join online communities, and network with professionals.
  • Job Boards: Regularly check job boards like LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor.

Succeeding in Interviews

  • Practice Coding Challenges: Use platforms like LeetCode, HackerRank, and CodeSignal.
  • Understand Technical Concepts: Be ready to explain your projects, discuss frameworks, and answer technical questions.

Mock Interviews

  • Practice with friends or use services like Pramp and Interviewing.io.
